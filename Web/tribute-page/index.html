Paul Walker

A legend that never dies...


Picture of Paul Walker in front of a blue Nissan Skyline GT-R (R34) - Fast and Furious 4


  • 1973: Paul William Walker IV was born in Glendale, California to American Parents with British, Swiss and Jewish ancestry. His mother was a fashion model and his father was a sewer contractor.

  • 1975: First appearance in TV in an ad for the company "Pampers".

  • 1984: Notable appearance on Showbiz Pizza TV ad, starts acting as part of TV Series "CBS Schoolbreak Special".

  • 1987: Securing first lead role in sitcom show "Throb" as Jeremy Beatty.

  • 1994: After a break from acting, walker began to the film industry, starring in Tammy and the T-Rex

  • 2001: Breakthrough appearance alongside Vin Diesel on the movie "The Fast and the Furious" (Universal Pictures).

  • 2003: Appearance in sequel for the Fast and The Furious, "2Fast 2 Furious".

  • 2010: Walker starred in TV Show "Tankers".

  • 2011: Paul Walker chosen as lead actor in MTV Teen Choice awards for the movie Fast 6.

  • 2013: On November 30, 2013, at about 3:30 p.m. PST, Walker, 40, and Roger Rodas, 38, left an event for Walker's charity Reach Out Worldwide for victims of Typhoon Haiyan (Yolanda), with Rodas driving his 2005 Porsche Carrera GT. In a 45 mph (72 km/h) speed zone on Hercules Street near Kelly Johnson Parkway in Valencia, Santa Clarita, California, the car crashed into a concrete lamp post and two trees subsequently catching fire to the vehicle. Walker died from combined effects of Trauma and Death.
